How it works

Three steps. That's it.

No setup hassle. Pick your toys, get alerts, go grab them.

1

Pick what you're hunting

Choose from presets like NeeDoh, Pokémon TCG, or Squishmallows — or paste any product link from a supported retailer.

2

Get alerted instantly

We scan 6 retailers every hour (every 30 minutes with Premium). The moment your item restocks, you get a push notification with the store and location.

3

Go grab it

Open the map, see which stores near you have stock, get directions, and confirm the find for fellow hunters.
